What Is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a tool that takes your birthdate and calculates your exact age. It doesn't just tell you your age in years — it breaks it down into years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

How to Use

1. Click the "Calculate My Age" button above
2. Enter your date of birth (day, month, year)
3. Click Calculate
4. See your comprehensive age breakdown

Why Use an Age Calculator?

Age calculators are useful for: school projects and assignments, legal documents and forms, medical records and appointments, insurance calculations, retirement planning, and simply satisfying curiosity about exactly how old you are.
