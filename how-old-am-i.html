Age in Different Units

Your age can be expressed in different units:

• Years: The standard way to measure age
• Months: Useful for baby tracking and school enrollment
• Weeks: Fun to know how many weeks you've lived
• Days: Precise and tangible measurement
• Hours: Shows the vast amount of time you've experienced
• Minutes: Over 15 million minutes for a 30-year-old
• Seconds: Nearly 1 billion seconds for a 30-year-old

Fun Age Facts

The average person lives about 70 years, which is approximately 25,550 days, 613,200 hours, or 36.8 million minutes. Your heart beats about 100,000 times per day, so by age 30, your heart has beaten over 1 billion times.
