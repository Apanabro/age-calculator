What Is My Exact Age?

Most people know their age in years, but few know their exact age in months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Age Master gives you a complete age breakdown that's both informative and fascinating.

How to Find Your Age

Simply enter your date of birth in the calculator above and click Calculate. The results will show:

• Age in years, months, and days
• Total weeks lived
• Total days lived
• Total hours lived
• Total minutes lived
• Total seconds lived

Fun Age Facts

The average person takes about 700,000 breaths per year. A 30-year-old has taken approximately 21 million breaths. Your heart beats about 100,000 times per day. By 30, your heart has beaten over 1 billion times.
