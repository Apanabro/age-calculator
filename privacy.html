1. Introduction

Welcome to Age Master ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the Age Master web application available at apanabro.github.io/age-calculator/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our age calculator tool and related services.

By using Age Master, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, date of birth, and email address — only when you voluntarily provide them by creating an account or signing up.
  • Usage Data: Non-personal information automatically collected when you use the app, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on the application.
  • Age Calculation Data: Date of birth and name entered into the calculator are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers unless you have an active account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Age Master service, including age calculations and birthday countdowns.
  • Personalize your experience and save your preferences locally on your device.
  • Process account creation and authentication for registered users.
  • Improve, optimize, and develop new features for the application.
  • Monitor usage trends and analyze aggregated data to enhance performance.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, or support messages.

4. Local Storage

Age Master uses localStorage on your device to store preferences, settings, and recent calculations. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings. Local storage allows us to remember your theme preferences, saved calculations, and app configuration without requiring an account.

5. Data Storage and Backend

For registered users, we store account information — including your name, email address, and password (hashed) — in MongoDB, a cloud-hosted database service. Our backend is deployed on Render.com, which provides secure, encrypted infrastructure. All data transmitted between your browser and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze usage patterns. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities of the application.

We may use Google Analytics or similar analytics services to collect anonymized usage data. These services help us understand how users interact with Age Master so we can improve the experience. Google Analytics operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Third-Party Services

Age Master relies on the following third-party services:

  • Render.com — Backend hosting and API deployment.
  • MongoDB Atlas — Cloud database for user account data.
  • GitHub Pages — Static site hosting for the front-end application.
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery for the Inter typeface used throughout the application.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymized usage analytics (if enabled).

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

8. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you individually. We may disclose your information only when required by law, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
