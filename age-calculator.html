What Is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a tool that calculates your exact age from your date of birth. It shows your age in years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Our free online age calculator provides the most accurate results, accounting for leap years and varying month lengths.

Age Calculator Formula

The basic age formula is: Current Date - Date of Birth = Age. However, accurate calculation requires accounting for leap years (every 4 years) and varying month lengths (28-31 days). Our calculator handles all these complexities automatically to give you the most precise result.
