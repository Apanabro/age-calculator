What Is an Exact Age Calculator?

Unlike a simple age calculator that only shows years, an exact age calculator breaks down your age into every possible unit: years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. It gives you the most precise measurement of your time on Earth.

How It Works

The calculator uses your date of birth and today's date to compute: complete years, months and remaining days, total weeks, total days, total hours, total minutes, and total seconds since birth.

Why Exact Age Matters

Legal documents often require exact age. Medical records track precise age for developmental milestones. Insurance policies calculate premiums based on exact age. And for personal curiosity, knowing your age in seconds is fascinating.
