Find Your Age in Weeks

Some people prefer to measure age in weeks rather than years. A 30-year-old person has lived approximately 1,560 weeks. This calculator gives you the exact number of weeks since your birth.

How Weeks Are Calculated

The calculator divides your total days alive by 7 to get complete weeks, then shows remaining days. For example: 1,095 total days = 156 weeks and 3 days.

Fun Week Facts

The average person lives about 4,000 weeks. A 50-year-old has lived approximately 2,600 weeks. A 25-year-old has lived about 1,300 weeks. Knowing your age in weeks can give you a new perspective on life.
